### Basic Words:
- Hombre = Man
- Mujer = Woman
- Nino = Boy
- Nina = Girl
- El = He
- Ella = She
- Un = A for masculine
- Una = A for feminine
- Tu = You
- Eres = are 


### Basic Sentences:
- Tu eres una mujer = You are a woman.
- Tu eres un hombre = You are a man.
- Ella es una mujer = She is a woman.
- El es un hombre = He is a man.
- Tu eres un nino = You are a boy.
- Tu eres una nina = You are a girl.
