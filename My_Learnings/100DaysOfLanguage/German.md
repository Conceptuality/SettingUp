### Basic Words:
- Du = You
- und = And
- Ich = I
- Frau = Woman
- Mann = Man
- Kind = Child
- Madchen = Girl
- Junge = Boy
- Hallo = Hello
- Brot = Bread
- Milch = Milk
- Wasser = Water
- bin = am
- bist = are
- ein/eine = a (eine is used for Woman others it is ein)

### Basic Sentences:
- Hallo, ich bin Junge. = Hello, I am a boy.
- Brot und Milch = Bread and Milk.
- Ich bin ein Mann, du bist eine Frau = I am a man, you are a woman.
